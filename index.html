Our
mission

Here on Wild at Risk, our mission is to raise awareness about the plight of endangered species around the world.

We believe that every creature has value and deserves to be protected and preserved for future generations.

Our website features individual profiles of endangered animals, highlighting their unique characteristics, habitats, and the threats they face.

We hope that by sharing this information, we can inspire people to take action and help protect endangered species.
